<Social Media Do's for College Students>

1.Do use privacy settings to manage who can see your personal posts and information. 

2.Do think critically before posting, as digital footprints are permanent and searchable. 

3.Do use professional language when engaging with faculty, seniors, or industry professionals. 

4.Do verify the authenticity of news or information before sharing it with others. 

5.Do use platforms like LinkedIn to build a professional network and showcase your skills. 


<Social Media Don'ts for College Students>

1.Don't post sensitive personal details like your home address, phone number, or ID cards. 

2.Don't engage in cyberbullying, harassment, or the spreading of hate speech. 

3.Don't share your passwords, OTPs, or financial details on any social platform. 

4.Don't post content that portrays illegal activities or unprofessional behavior. 

5.Don't tag others in photos or posts without their explicit permission.
